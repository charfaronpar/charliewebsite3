Expoloring the Supply Chain World With an Emphasis on Service

Open to New Experiences

Working at Vivint Smart Home Inc.'s distribution center in Lindon, UT, provided me with invaluable insights into the logistics and operations of a large-scale consumer electronics company. I gained hands-on experience in managing inventory, coordinating shipments, and ensuring timely delivery of products to customers across the country. This role sharpened my organizational skills and deepened my understanding of supply chain dynamics in a fast-paced environment. Additionally, my time at Marvellous Catering in Provo, UT, allowed me to apply these skills in a different context, focusing on food distribution and event logistics. Here, I honed my ability to manage perishable goods, coordinate catering services for various events, and deliver exceptional customer experiences. These combined experiences have equipped me with a diverse skill set in logistics, operations management, and customer service, preparing me to excel in dynamic and customer-centric industries.
