Education

BYU Marriot School of Business
Global Supply Chain Management 2026

The Marriott School of Business's Global Supply Chain Management program equips students with a comprehensive understanding of the intricate networks that drive global commerce. Through rigorous coursework and hands-on learning experiences, students delve into the strategic, operational, and logistical aspects of supply chain management. They learn to analyze and optimize supply chains to enhance efficiency, reduce costs, and mitigate risks across international markets. The curriculum emphasizes sustainable practices, innovative technologies, and cross-cultural communication skills essential for navigating today's interconnected global economy. Graduates emerge prepared to lead in diverse industries, leveraging their expertise to innovate and streamline supply chain operations on a global scale.
